About Linkin Park

Linkin Park is an American rock band from Agoura Hills, California. The band's current lineup comprises:

  • Mike Shinoda – Vocals, guitar
  • Brad Delson – Guitar
  • Rob Bourdon – Drums
  • Dave Farrell – Bass guitar
  • Joe Hahn – Turntables

The band has achieved great success since their debut album, Hybrid Theory, was released in 2000.

Famous Albums

  • Hybrid Theory (2000)
  • Meteora (2003)
  • Minutes to Midnight (2007)

Over the years, Linkin Park has produced chart-topping songs and retired several of them in live performances.
