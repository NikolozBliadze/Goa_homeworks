Three Amazing Animals

  • Lion
  • Elephant
  • Penguin

Lion

The lion, known as the king of the jungle, is a majestic and powerful animal that lives in the grasslands of Africa. Lions live in groups called prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals in the world. They are known for their intelligence, memory, and long trunks, which they use to grab food and water.

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that live primarily in the Southern Hemisphere. They are excellent swimmers and use their wings like flippers to move in water.
